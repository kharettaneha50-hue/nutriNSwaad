Our Story

NutriNova - Bringing Health to Your Table

NutriNova was born out of a simple desire: to make healthy snacking accessible and delicious. We noticed a gap in the market for snacks that truly prioritized nutrition without compromising on taste.

With our Nutrinswaad line, we bring you the ancient wisdom of superfoods like ragi and quinoa, combined with nutrient-dense nuts and seeds. Our jams and marmalades are a labor of love, crafted to preserve the natural sweetness of fresh fruits, completely free from artificial preservatives and excessive sugars.

Join us on a journey to better health, one delicious bite at a time.
